Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How accurate is this Pakistan tax calculator? +

Our calculator is 100% accurate and complies with the latest FBR (Federal Board of Revenue) tax slabs as per Finance Act 2025. It includes all tax rates, surcharges, and exemptions for the 2025-26 tax year.

What is the tax-free limit in Pakistan for 2025-26? +

The tax-free income limit in Pakistan for 2025-26 is Rs. 600,000 per year. If your annual income is below this amount, you don't need to pay any income tax.

How do I calculate my monthly tax from yearly income? +

Simply divide your annual tax liability by 12 months. Our calculator automatically shows both monthly and yearly tax breakdowns for your convenience.

What is the highest tax rate in Pakistan? +

The highest income tax rate in Pakistan is 35% for income exceeding Rs. 4,100,000. Additionally, a 9% surcharge applies if your annual income exceeds Rs. 10 million.

Does this calculator include provincial taxes? +

This calculator focuses on federal income tax as per FBR guidelines. Provincial taxes and other local taxes are not included in these calculations.

Can I use this for business income tax calculation? +

This calculator is specifically designed for salaried individuals. Business income has different tax slabs and rules which are not covered by this tool.
